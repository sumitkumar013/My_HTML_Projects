Geography of Vaishali

Vaishali is located in the northern part of Bihar and is part of the Gangetic plains. It is surrounded by the Gandak River on the west and the Ganges River to the south. The district lies between latitudes 25°35'N to 26°13'N and longitudes 85°10'E to 85°45'E.

The terrain is primarily alluvial with fertile soil, making it suitable for agriculture. The climate is tropical with hot summers and mild winters. The monsoon season brings significant rainfall, especially between June and September.
