About Chennai Super Kings

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK), one of the most successful franchises in IPL history, are known for their consistency and iconic captain MS Dhoni. Based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and representing the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, the team has won multiple IPL titles and is adored by fans worldwide.
