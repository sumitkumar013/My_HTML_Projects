About Volleyball

Invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan, volleyball is now an Olympic sport played by millions worldwide. The game is played between two teams of six players, and the objective is to send the ball over the net and ground it on the opponent's side while preventing the same on your side.

Volleyball promotes athleticism, teamwork, and strategy. With different variations such as beach volleyball, indoor volleyball, and sitting volleyball, the sport continues to grow in popularity.
