Demography of Vaishali

As per the 2011 Census of India, Vaishali district has a population of around 3.5 million. It is predominantly rural, with only a small percentage living in urban areas. The district has a sex ratio of about 895 females for every 1,000 males.

The literacy rate is approximately 66%, with male literacy around 75% and female literacy about 55%. The district is home to various communities and religious groups, with Hindus and Muslims forming the majority.
